    About Us

    A story of success that rose above geographical boundaries.

    A global holding company with a portfolio of investments that started in the Balkans then began to expand steadily through the Middle East, Central and South Europe, and the USA.

    Our Story

    It all started from Greece where the Group’s Chiarmain, the dynamic entrepreneur Mr. Petros Stathis, started his successful journey with a consulting company that worked with the biggest enterprises in the country. In 2000, Mr. Stathis met his Montenegrin wife and moved to the vibrant country of Montenegro; a stepping stone for his future success.

    Mr. Stathis started exploring the market opportunities in Montenegro, embracing his exceptional experience and ambitious vision. Through the years that followed, with passion, patience, flexibility, hard work, and persistence, Mr. Stathis had set out himself to become one of the top entrepreneurs in the region, sourcing new investment opportunities in various fields including Real Estate, Hospitality, Banking, Media, and Food Industry.

    Out of the dream to consolidate the different investments of Mr. Stathis under one corporate umbrella, MonteRock International was born.

    Mission

    We grow by being inventive and daring.

    The first step of our mission was to discover the possible opportunities within the country of Montenegro, backing up the experience and knowledge in hospitality and real estate to add more investments for a long-term value enhancement to the group. MRI is now a pioneer of a private equity type of investments , embracing different businesses such as media, real estate, food and agriculture, banking, hospitality, industrial technology and production.

    Backing our steps with determination, we succeeded to strike a balance between short-term and long-term strategies in order to achieve profitable organic growth in the selected businesses. MRI now aims to increase the umbrella of its investments and operate across several other countries, in order to boost its range of interests by focusing on a value-creation approach and developing commercial excellence capabilities.

    MRI is always eager to take an active position in distinctive businesses that will span the globe by investing in the firm itself for the long-term and sharing the success of our portfolio companies with our partners/managers.

    From our belief that success is also about being an active member of the community and having a more considerate role in a wider world, our Group’s Chairman Mr. Petros Stathis has started an initiative with the assistance of one of MRI investments: UCB Bank Podgorica. Both managed to sponsor a number of orphans by offering them bank deposits with the highest interest rates, which they can use after leaving the orphanage at the age of 18. In addition to this , Mr. Stathis and his family has shown social solidarity with the local community and contributed to the health sector in Montenegro by donating funds and medical equipment on several occasions - a step that speaks loudly for the values he believes in.

    Our Vision

    MRI has a goal to shoot for.

    When it comes to our vision and how we see our company in the future, we are always striving for accomplishing more.
    Out of our desire to become one of the most reputable corporations in the world, while increasing the value of our investment portfolio, we are challenging ourselves to create a long-lasting value chain and deliver organic growth by increasing the reach of our geographical presence, the utilization of diverse technology means, the scale of multiple businesses interconnections as well as investing in our corporation itself.
    At MRI, we always keep an eye on today’s opportunities while building a strong foundation for tomorrow’s achievements, with realistic aspirations to be an international holding company that entails a paramount success while adopting a private equity approach.
    Our ambitious vision is not only about increasing the number of our investments, but also to create a legacy. Such strive for success and innovative vision is adopted by every member company of our Holding , where everyone is committed to excellence and uniqueness.

    Our Values

    At MRI, we champion the value of working with passion, patience, persistence, and staying true to who we are.

    In order to stay true to who we are, from the first day we embarked on our journey, we knew that boosting ourselves to the top will not happen without remembering our roots. It’s no coincidence that we decided that the first part of our company’s name is to be “Monte’’, associating to the roots of our origin. We believe such name will always remind us of how we started and the philosophy we have adopted throughout our journey.
    On the other hand, MRI nurtures the culture of embracing long-term business opportunities across different geographical areas and cultures. More than 70 nationalities are represented within the Human Capital of our Group and each one of them has greatly contributed to shape the betterment of MRI achievements.
    We truly believe the “world is flat” and that the rewards of our intended globalized reach will outweigh the risks.
